About Us

Hebron integrated Farms and Mills Ltd is an agro-based solution company with interest in rice production, processing and marketing, poultry production and feed milling. We are an agro initiative with a unified aim of leveraging on the on-going agricultural revolution in the country to carve a niche for ourselves in rice production, poultry production, and feed milling, thereby becoming a household name and renowned supplier of these agro products.

“It’s quite easy to feed the nation. All we need are like-minded people willing to invest in agriculture.”

Alphonsus Inyang, Chairman/CEO

At Hebron, we run a four policy program of producing, processing, marketing and impacting.

The Company

Hebron Group was registered with the Nigerian Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) in 2013. In the last six years, we have evolved into an integrated Agro business and services magnate operating in Nigeria with Africa as its focus. Our business approach is reflected in our slogan- “Structured Ingenuity”, which articulates our resolution and ability to deliver products and services based on tested methodology that reflects our ethos of overcoming challenges with innovative solutions.

The Vision

At Hebron, our vision is to develop and provide quality agricultural products and services that exceed the expectations of our esteemed customers and to build long term relationships with our out-growers and customers through production, processing, and marketing agricultural products through innovation and advanced technology.

We train local farmers to create wealth through agriculture; we generate employment opportunities for the locals, we lead the massive increase in food production and anchor the spread of prosperity to rural communities.

What we do

With more than 2,500 hectares of land seeded and ready for milling, Hebron Rice is ready to take over the Nigerian rice market with superior quality, well-packaged and affordable rice that will meet the demands of yearning Nigerians, even while keeping to excellent nutritional standards.

Our feed mill equipment is state-of-the-art equipment for feed milling animals such as poultry and livestock. The equipment is handled by well-trained expertise to ensure quality compliance. Our 110 metric tons per day of A-grade feed mill can sufficiently supply and sustain any viable livestock and poultry production enterprise.

Our modern poultry equipment and feeding systems ensure our chickens and eggs are of the finest quality. Our poultry farm is capable of producing over 20,000 tons of poultry meat and eggs per year and is expected to increase production to 30, 000 tons thereby earning you an all year round supply of your in-demand product – be it chicken or egg.

Mission

Mr. Inyang, our CEO is passionate about feeding the masses, defeating poverty and alleviating hunger through the utilization of local labor throughout our production processes and the support of local farmers via the Outgrower and the Anchor Borrowers Scheme in partnership with Bank of Agriculture and the Central Bank of Nigeria.

Hebron Integrated Farms and Mills Ltd is run by a team of passionate and innovative young people who deliver integrated solutions and quality agricultural management system that sets Hebron miles apart from its competitors and headed by Mr. Alphonsus Inyang, an Agricultural Development Expert with more than 30 years of experience in the field of agro-businesses.
