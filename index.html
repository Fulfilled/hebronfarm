Welcome to Hebron Farms

Your one-stop source for consolidated agro products and services.

At Hebron, we bring to you an integrated farm where your food and agro needs are met with our home-grown and processed fine quality and affordable rice; a poultry farm with high class and plush chickens that fits your top dietary demand, in addition to a feed mill that produces 110 metric tons of A-grade feeds per day.

We are focused on providing best agro products and services with the highest level of customer satisfaction. With a variety of offerings to choose from, we are certain you will be happy patronizing us. Explore our website and discover our excellent agro products and services.

FEED-MILL

Our feed mill is state-of-the-art equipment for feed milling animals such as poultry and livestock. The equipment is handled by well-trained expertise to ensure quality compliance. We have many years of experience in producing high quality feed supplement for poultry and livestock.

POULTRY

Our modern poultry equipment and feeding systems ensures our chickens and eggs are of the finest quality. We deal on quality broilers and layers that are expertly nurtured to mediate with your excellent nutritional tastes.

RICE

We are set to be one of the esteemed and trusted rice producers in Nigeria. At Hebron, we take pride in the production and supply of the best quality of local rice. We understand your needs for affordable, yet quality rice and we are out to accomplish just that!
