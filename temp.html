About Us

Hebron integrated Farms and Mills Ltd is a local agro-based solution industry that spans across the sectors of rice production and marketing, poultry production and feed milling. Hebron is an agro initiative with a unified aim of leveraging on the current rice revolution in the country and carving a niche market for rice production, thereby becoming a household name and renowned supplier of this staple food to the Nigerian populace and beyond.

Hebron Integrated Farms and Mills Ltd was borne out of the necessity to provide succour and alleviate the diminishing circulation of rice within the country borders following the cutback in rice importation, while also integrating poultry production and feed milling to render a holistic agro products and services that will compete favourably in the local market. Our home-grown rice is expected to contribute 15, 000 tonnes per hectares of rice that will supply the local market all year round, combined with the integrated mill that will produce 110 metric tons per day.

Mr Alphonsus Inyang, an agricultural development expert and the Managing Director of Hebron Integrated Farms and Mills Ltd is passionate about feeding the masses, alleviating hunger and defeating poverty through the utilisation of local labour throughout its production process, hence, Hebron iniative employs over 2,000 local farmers across the three states of Akwa Ibom, Benue and Nasarawa.

At Hebron, we run a four policy programme of producing, processing, marketing and impacting. We train and deploy local farmers to create wealth through agriculture, aided by soft loans through the Anchor Borrowers Scheme in partnership with Bank of Agriculture and the Central Bank of Nigeria. We generate employment opportunities for the locals and we lead the massive increase in food production and anchor the spread of prosperity to rural communities. And more so, we're looking forward to spreading our tentacles to including the outgrower scheme where women and youths produce for Hebron to offtake from them.

With more than 2,500 hectares of land seeded and ready for milling, Hebron Rice is ready to take over the Nigerian rice market with a superior quality, well-packaged and affordable rice that will meet the demands of yearning Nigerians, even while keeping to excellent nutritional standards.
